    Help - Client Card

    Your Client Card is the 16-digit card you use for debit and ATM transactions. You can also use the number you were given at the branch to access online banking.

    A username is a unique ID that you create to access your RBC Royal Bank Online Banking account.

    Help - Remember Me

    Remember me is a secure and convenient way to sign into RBC Royal Bank Online Banking. We don't recommend this option if you're using a public or shared computer.

    To turn this feature on, select the 'Remember me' box and a cookie will allow RBC to recognize your computer the next time you sign in. If you delete cookies on your computer you will erase the identification(s) you have saved.
